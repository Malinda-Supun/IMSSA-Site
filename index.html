About Us

Students, encouraged and assisted by the academic staff, have formed the “Industrial Management Science Students Association” (IMSSA), one of the most dynamic student bodies in the Faculty of Science. Its main objective is to provide the students with an opportunity to develop their leadership, communication and group skills through design, development and implementation of selected projects. In addition, it supplements the learning of its members by organizing lectures, field visits etc. It also provides the opportunity for members to interact socially among themselves and with the staff through its CSR projects and annual trips.

Annual Pirith Ceremoney

The annual Pirith Ceremony & the Alms giving organized by the Industrial Management Science Students' Association was successfully held on 16th and 17th of November 2018 at the department premises. The main purpose of organizing this ceremony was to bless all the undergraduates as well as the academic & the non-academic staff of the faculty of Science. The pirith ceremony continued from 16th Friday evening till 17th Saturday morning. The alms giving was served for the theros & the all who took part in the event in Saturday morning. With the participation of many faculty staff members & undergraduates the whole event concluded as a success.

IM Car Wash

The Annual IM Car Wash organized by the Industrial Management Science Students' Association was successfully held on 14th July 2018 from 8am onwards at Salinda Super car park, Mahara , Kadawatha. The main purpose of organizing this event was to raise funds for the upcoming events of IMSSA and to get a different yet fun experience for the undergraduates apart from everyday academic routine. Head of the department, the academic staff, non-academic staff, and all the undergraduates of department of Industrial Management joined for the success of the event. Some tickets were printed before the event to sell for the purpose of fund raising. Also car stickers were sold during the event. The event concluded successfully reaching the targets of the team.

Dansela

The Annual 'IM Dansala' organized by Industrial Management Science Students' Association was held on 30th of May at the Science Faculty entrance of University of Kelaniya. 500kg of Manioc with kochchi sambol was distributed over several hours among the university undergraduates & staff as well as the people who were passing by. The main purpose of organizing this event was to get the opportunity of doing a social activity during the Vesak religious festival commemorating events of significance of Lord Buddha. Enhancing the mutual understanding, improving the unity among the undergraduates by working as a team, improving the organizing skills of the undergraduates were some other special objectives of holding this kind of event. Also this event leads them to get an understanding on how the undergraduates are expected to undertake responsibilities towards the society.
